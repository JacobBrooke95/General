Now

A now page of my current projects, learning, and focus areas

Work

Assistant Director for Planning at Missouri's Office of Administration, Budget & Planning Division. Day-to-day:

  • Leading a 25-person team of analysts that supports the Governor's Office on legislation and policy
  • Tracking the post-session bill review window — ~60 passed bills to work through by June 26
  • Setting AI-implementation strategy for the division — figuring out where Claude actually earns its keep in state government workflows
  • Managing internal budget, HR, and long-term planning for the office

Projects

Side projects I'm building or maintaining, mostly with Claude as the co-author:

  • Zen Garden: A pixel-art ambient scene that runs on Apple TV and reacts to live weather data. Self-hosted on a Mac and tunneled out via Tailscale.
  • Home Camera AI: A self-hosted security-camera dashboard that uses vision models to triage motion events into a webapp instead of a flood of notifications.
  • Pebble × Claude: Connecting a Pebble smartwatch to Claude as a quick-capture and ambient-glance interface.
  • Reconnecting Jefferson City: A long-running project tracking the riverfront walkway and downtown connectivity for the capital city.
  • Personal vault: An Obsidian-based second brain with Claude as a persistent thinking partner — daily briefings, research, and capture pipelines.
  • This site: A small personal site I rebuild whenever I want to test a Claude-driven workflow against something real.

Learning

What I'm actively trying to get better at:

  • Local-first AI: Running models, voice synthesis, and image generation on a Mac instead of leaning on cloud APIs for every call
  • Self-hosted infrastructure: Caddy, Tailscale Funnel, launchd, and the patience to make all three behave
  • Agent design: Writing skills, hooks, and subagents for Claude so that the routine parts of my week run themselves
  • Public-sector AI policy: Reading the literature so the implementation work at the office has good ground under it

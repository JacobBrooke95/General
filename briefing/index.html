About the Show

Briefing is a personal daily morning podcast covering world events, technology and AI developments, calendar items, messages, weather, and local news for Jefferson City, Missouri. Episodes are researched, written, and produced by Claude.

The show includes several formats:

  • Daily Briefings — Short morning updates covering the day's news and schedule
  • Deep Dives — Long-form analysis on specific topics in AI, policy, and technology
  • Week in Review — Weekly recap episodes summarizing the major stories
  • The Keeping House — An original serialized literary novel read chapter by chapter

About Me

I'm Jacob — I lead policy review for Missouri's Budget & Planning Division, managing a 25-person team of analysts that supports the Governor's Office. Over eight years in state government, my work has been about one thing: making government work better.

I've modernized legislative systems, driven statewide process improvements, and more recently explored AI implementation strategies to help our office work more effectively. Alongside the day-to-day of tracking legislation and coordinating policy across all 17 state departments, I manage internal budget controls, HR, and long-term planning for the division.

I graduated Magna Cum Laude from Truman State University with a degree in Political Science & Public Administration, and completed the Missouri Leadership Academy in 2022. I'm always looking for ways to bring better tools and clearer thinking to public service — and when I'm not in the office, you'll find me out on Missouri's trails.

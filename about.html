About Me

I'm Jacob, and I've spent over seven years in Missouri's legislative process working with the state's Budget & Planning division. What started as a Political Science degree from Truman State turned into a career I'm passionate about - helping make government work better.

My work involves leading a team of analysts, managing legislative information flow between state agencies and the Governor's Office, and ensuring policy proposals get thorough review. I helped Missouri modernize our fiscal note review process by transitioning to a streamlined web-based platform.

I'm a problem-solver who turns complicated policy challenges into clear insights. I've shared expertise with NASBO on "Smart Brevity" communication techniques. When I'm not working on legislation, you'll find me exploring Missouri's trails.
